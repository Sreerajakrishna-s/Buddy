ABOUT COMFORT


Located in central Chennai, Lemon Tree Hotel offers 4-star accommodations with a seating area and flat-screen TV. The hotel features free parking, a 24-hour restaurant and well-equipped fitness center. Chennai Trade Center is 3.7 mi and Phenix Market City is 2.5 mi from the hotel. The air-conditioned rooms at Lemon Tree Hotel Chennai come with modern décor and hardwood flooring. Each well-lit room is fitted with a mini-bar and private bathroom with a bathtub. Guests can work out at the gym or swim in the outdoor pool. For convenience, the hotel also provides currency exchange services and free parking. Citrus Café serves a daily buffet spread of Indian and international dishes, while light snacks and refreshing cocktails are offered at Slounge.
